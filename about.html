About me

Creativity and curiosity have been the drive forces that fueled my professional career for almost a decade. I started doing branding and content work for big brands like Netflix, Spotify and Warner Bros., but for the past few years I fell in love with product design as i believe it's the perfect combination between my love for technology and storytelling. Working in the design field allowed me to understand user pain points, draft different solutions and bring concepts to life, I developed new skills that make me what I believe a professional today, not only technical ones, but also those that show how companies work, how projects and deadlines should be managed and how to communicate effectively with managers and clients.

I am passionate about making a positive impact in everything I do, and I always strive to find solutions that can shape the future of how humans can behave with each other, with technology and the world around them.

On a personal note, you will hardly catch me without a smile on my face - it's kind of my thing!

My core values

No. 1

Work together

I believe everything is better when shared. Collaborative processes spark innovation and help us find better solutions to create products that resonate well.

No. 2

Keep it simple

My workflow aims to avoid overcomplication, overselling, and never-ending processes. I bring solutions that solve problems with clear and direct design choices that are easy to follow.

No. 3

Users First

Creating durable, sustainable solutions often requires thinking in the long term and considering unknown paths. This perspective helps me create products that are future-proof, removed from ego, but that the end users love.

No. 4

Honesty is all

Good relationships are based on trust. To build these relationships, I strive to be honest and transparent with my teammates and clients who are part of the project we're working on together.
